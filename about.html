Our Vision

With a life-cycle approach to development, behave as a catalyst in bringing lasting change to the lives of poor children, youth and women.


Make it possible for society to participate actively in the change process.

Our Mission

Empower poor children, youth and women through appropiate education and innovative healthcare programs.
